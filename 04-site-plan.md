## Low / Expected / High Recommendations

### Low case — 0.5 acre (~21,780 sqft / ~2,023 sqm)

- Compressed layout.
- 40-ft (12 m) weighbridge **only** (accepts losing multi-axle market).
- 4 EV bays (no growth pad).
- 1,400 sqft coffee shop (indoor seating + single restroom block).
- **Works only if** the plot is a corner/T-junction with two natural access roads so you get segregation free.
- Higher risk: panel's Kill Criterion #1 says this is the minimum viable plot.

### Expected case — 0.65–0.75 acre (~28,300–32,670 sqft / ~2,630–3,035 sqm) ← **recommended**

- 60-ft (18 m) weighbridge, back-of-plot, dedicated HV entry.
- 6 EV bays + growth pad to 8.
- 1,750 sqft coffee shop with twin restroom blocks.
- 5 m segregation buffer.
- Dual highway access (HV and LV entries separated by 40 m frontage).
- **This is the sweet spot** — clears the panel's segregation requirement, supports the full-truck-mix weighbridge, and gives the hospitality side room to breathe.

### High case — 1.0 acre (~43,560 sqft / ~4,046 sqm)

- Same zone specs as expected case, but with:
  - 8 EV bays at go-live, pad for 12 (future-proofs 2030 demand).
  - Extended outdoor seating / kids' area / garden (raises family-car conversion).
  - Back-of-plot godown or small truck-parking overnight (second revenue line for long-haul truckers).
  - Residual expansion pad for a future 2nd weighbridge or axle-weigher.
- **Biggest benefit:** panel's Taleb criterion — residual land value if all three businesses fail. 1 acre is an easier exit as godown / petrol-pump / plotted-residential than 0.5 acre.

## Final Recommendations

1. **Target plot size: 0.75 acre (~32,670 sqft / ~3,035 sqm).** This clears segregation, supports 60-ft weighbridge, and gives EV and F&B room to scale.
2. **Weighbridge: 60-ft (18 m), 80-ton, pitless, back-of-plot.** Not 40-ft. The ₹5 L you save by going 40-ft costs you the multi-axle market forever.
3. **EV charging: 6 bays at go-live (4 DC fast + 2 AC), pad for 8.** Partner with a named CPO (Tata Power / Statiq / ChargeZone / Ather) under revenue-share — do not self-operate chargers.
4. **Coffee shop: ~1,750 sqft two-counter design** (premium side for cars/EVs, trucker side for HV drivers), with twin restroom blocks. Restrooms are the real differentiator.
5. **Highway frontage minimum: ~65 m**, ideally a corner plot with two access roads so segregation is architectural, not forced.
6. **Total capex envelope: ₹2.0–2.3 Cr** including land (expected case).
7. **Kill-criterion reminders from the panel:** do not buy land without (a) NHAI 2030 master plan check, (b) CPO site-LOI in hand, (c) 4-week manual truck count at the specific plot, (d) father's written 5-year commitment, (e) residual-use appraisal if all three businesses fail.
