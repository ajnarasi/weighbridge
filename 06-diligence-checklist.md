# Diligence Checklist — Before Any Land Purchase

Use this as the field-visit checklist you and your father take on every candidate plot. Do not sign an advance / MoU on any plot that fails more than one of the **kill criteria**.

---

## A. Kill Criteria — any YES here = walk away

- [ ] Plot is **< 0.5 acre** — segregation impossible
- [ ] Plot requires **shared HV/LV access** (single gate)
- [ ] NHAI / NHDP master plan shows a **bypass, re-alignment, or expressway corridor shift within 5 km** in next 10 years
- [ ] No named CPO (Tata Power, Statiq, ChargeZone, Ather, Jio-bp) will commit to a **site LOI before land purchase**
- [ ] Total envelope (land + capex + 3-yr op reserve) exceeds **40% of liquid net worth**
- [ ] Father's honest 5-year health / energy outlook cannot sustain **10–12-hour days for the first 24 months**
- [ ] Plot has **no residual use** (can't be sold as godown / petrol-pump site / plotted residential if venture fails)

---

## B. Traffic & Demand Diligence

- [ ] **4-week manual truck count** at the specific plot — Tuesday + Saturday + post-holiday Monday + a random Wednesday. Count: direction, axle class (rigid / multi-axle), and time-of-day distribution.
- [ ] **4-week light-vehicle count** on the same schedule — cars, SUVs, MUVs, two-wheelers (for locals).
- [ ] **Peak-hour count** (8–11 am and 4–7 pm) — your peak load.
- [ ] **Compare to NHAI AADT** data for the corridor — is your plot above or below average? Why?
- [ ] **Observe pull-off behaviour** — do vehicles actually slow and stop near this stretch, or just blow through?
- [ ] **Night-time count** for 2 nights — truck movement is often heavier after 10 pm on NH corridors.

---

## C. Competitor Weighbridge Survey (within 8 km radius)

For each existing weighbridge:

- [ ] Location + owner + years in operation
- [ ] Platform length and capacity (40 / 60 / 80 / 100-ton)
- [ ] **Pay for one weighment** — note fee, process time, slip format, staff attitude
- [ ] **Observe utilization** during RTO-check peak hour — trucks in queue, trucks turning away
- [ ] Presence of axle-weighers, axle-wise weighment capability
- [ ] Co-located services (dhaba, parking, fuel, mechanic)
- [ ] Ask drivers in the queue: "Why this one? Would you use another if closer?"

If you find ≥ 3 weighbridges within 8 km running <40% utilization → **market saturated**, drop to 40-ft captive-tenant model (or walk away from the weighbridge leg entirely).

---

## D. EV Charging Partnership Diligence

- [ ] **Pitch deck ready** for CPOs: location, traffic numbers, site plan, revenue-share ask.
- [ ] Schedule meetings with: **Tata Power EZ Charge, Statiq, ChargeZone, Ather Grid, Jio-bp Pulse, Glida, Zeon.**
- [ ] Get a **signed site LOI** from at least one CPO before paying advance on land.
- [ ] Confirm **landlord capex vs CPO capex split** — ideally CPO funds chargers, you fund civil + HT line.
- [ ] Confirm **revenue-share model** — typical range 15–25% to landlord, verify.
- [ ] Confirm **exclusivity clause** — can you host multiple CPOs to hedge?
- [ ] Check **Puducherry Electricity Dept (DISCOM) connection timeline** for 100–150 kW — some states quote 30 days, reality is 3–6 months.

---

## E. Land Legal & Regulatory Diligence

- [ ] **Title search** — 30-year encumbrance certificate from sub-registrar
- [ ] **Patta / chitta / khata** in seller's name, matches the survey number and extent
- [ ] **Parent document chain** — no break in conveyance
- [ ] **NA (non-agricultural) conversion status** — if agricultural, factor conversion time + fee
- [ ] **Puducherry Local Planning Authority zoning** — commercial use permitted?
- [ ] **Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) check** if anywhere near ECR — hard veto in many stretches
- [ ] **NHAI No-Objection Certificate** for access cuts on highway frontage — get pre-clearance before signing
- [ ] **Setback compliance**: 7.5 m from NH boundary, NOT within 40 m of NH centreline
- [ ] **Litigation search** — court records for the survey number
- [ ] **Adjoining-owner consent** for boundary wall and drainage
- [ ] **Groundwater availability** — commercial borewell permission from Puducherry Water Resources Dept

---

## F. Utility & Infrastructure Diligence

- [ ] **HT power availability** — 11 kV line within how many metres? Cost of extension?
- [ ] **Water** — borewell yield, TDS, seasonality
- [ ] **Drainage** — where does monsoon water flow? Is the plot on a low spot?
- [ ] **Sewage / septic** — public sewer available or on-site septic?
- [ ] **Internet / mobile** — 4G coverage for EV charger backend, weighbridge software, POS
- [ ] **Fire hydrant / fire station** within 5 km (insurance and NOC requirement)

---

## G. Operational Diligence

- [ ] **Labour availability** — can you hire 4–6 F&B staff + 2 weighbridge operators from nearby villages?
- [ ] **Local wage benchmarks** — Puducherry UT may differ from adjoining TN
- [ ] **Daily cash handling / banking** — nearest bank branch for daily deposit
- [ ] **Police station** — relationship matters on highway for night operations, break-in risk
- [ ] **RTO check post** — distance in either direction, which predicts weighbridge demand

---

## H. Father-Son Operating Contract (signed before land purchase)

Non-negotiable per Drucker's lens in [01-panel.md](01-panel.md).

- [ ] **Roles and decision rights** — who decides capex, hiring, supplier contracts?
- [ ] **Daily operating ownership** — father day-to-day, son weekly review?
- [ ] **Tie-breaker mechanism** — for disagreements, who wins and when?
- [ ] **Capital contribution split** and how future injections are treated
- [ ] **Profit distribution** — reinvestment % vs drawdown %
- [ ] **Exit mechanism** — what happens if one of you wants out at year 3 / 5 / 10?
- [ ] **Health contingency** — if father can't operate, what's the succession or closure plan?
- [ ] **Review cadence** — formal quarterly P&L review, annual business review
- [ ] **24-month kill-clause** on each of the three legs — pre-agreed criteria to shut any line that fails to clear its cost of capital

---

## I. Financial Diligence

- [ ] **Comparable recent sales** — last 3 highway-frontage transactions within 5 km, per-sqft price
- [ ] **Independent appraiser** — residual land value if all 3 businesses fail and plot sold as godown
- [ ] **10-year P&L model** with best / base / worst cases for each of the 3 lines
- [ ] **IRR comparison vs Nifty 50 index fund** for the same capital over 10 years — does the venture clear index + risk premium?
- [ ] **3-year operating reserve** in ring-fenced account before opening
- [ ] **Insurance**: fire, public liability, EV charger specific, business interruption, cyber (for POS and charger software)

---

## J. First 100 Days Operating Plan (draft before land purchase)

- [ ] **Soft launch** for 30 days to stabilize operations before any advertising
- [ ] **Google Business Profile + reviews** strategy from day 1
- [ ] **Trucker loyalty** program — frequent-weighment discount or logistics-company contract
- [ ] **EV-driver community** — partner with local EV owner groups for launch events
- [ ] **Weekly huddle** — staff check, customer complaints review, cash reconciliation
- [ ] **Monthly P&L** by line of business — so you see the weighbridge, EV, and F&B independently (prevents the Doom Loop Collins warned about)

---

## K. Lint Pass Before Signing Any Advance

Walk the plot one final time with this list:

1. Can a multi-axle 22-wheeler **enter the HV gate, traverse the weighbridge, turn around, and exit** without hitting anything? Physically walk the swept path.
2. From the highway at 60 km/h, **can you read the signage** for each customer segment in under 4 seconds?
3. From the coffee shop seating area, **can you see the weighbridge forecourt**? If yes, you haven't segregated enough.
4. In a 15-minute stand at the roadside, **how many cars vs trucks actually slow down**? Not "pass by" — slow down as if considering a stop.
5. Stand at the plot during a monsoon downpour if possible. **Where does water pool?**

---

## Summary — Decision Gates

| Gate | Decision |
|---|---|
| Any Section A kill-criterion = YES | **Walk away** |
| Plot passes A, but Section C shows saturated market | **Drop weighbridge leg OR drop plot** |
| Plot passes A and C, but no CPO LOI | **Delay land purchase until LOI in hand** |
| All of A, C, D, E pass | **Proceed to advance / MoU with 30-day due-diligence clause** |
| Registered sale deed | **Only after father-son contract (H) is signed** |
